About me

I'm a published poet and inspirational speaker. I speak at formal and informal events, and deliver educational talks at schools and universities. I'm fluent in English, Swahili, and of course KiChagga, my tribal language of Kilimanjaro region in Tanzania, where the famous Kilimanjaro mountain is found.
